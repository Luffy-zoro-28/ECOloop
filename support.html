Shipping Guidelines

Follow these detailed instructions to ensure your packaging returns arrive safely and contribute maximum environmental impact.

Preparation Checklist

  • Remove all personal labels and information
  • Clean containers (rinse if needed)
  • Sort by material type
  • Check eligibility with our scanner
  • Photograph items for tracking

Secure Packaging

  • Use sturdy cardboard boxes
  • Fill empty spaces with padding
  • Seal securely with tape
  • Weight limit: 50 lbs per box
  • Include packing list printout

Label & Ship

  • Print prepaid shipping label
  • Attach label securely to box
  • Schedule pickup or drop-off
  • Track shipment progress
  • Receive impact confirmation

Impact Verification Process

Transparency is at the heart of everything we do. Learn how we verify and track the environmental impact of every return to ensure credible, measurable results.

Our Verification Standards

Every return goes through a rigorous verification process using EPA-certified methodologies and third-party auditing to ensure accurate impact measurement.

EPA Certified Methodology

All impact calculations follow Environmental Protection Agency guidelines for waste reduction and carbon footprint measurement.

Real-Time Processing Data

Live tracking of material processing with weight verification, quality assessment, and recycling pathway documentation.

Third-Party Auditing

Independent verification by certified environmental auditors ensures accuracy and prevents greenwashing.

Blockchain Transparency

Immutable record-keeping using blockchain technology provides complete transparency and prevents data manipulation.
